March 2015 project

The Foundation partnered with Woodmont Hills Church in Nashville, TN to build an outdoor, lighted basketball court on the church property. Ty loved to play church league basketball and many friendships were made and deepened on the court. So, to continue building relationships we wanted to make it easy to play basketball. There are not many courts that are easily accessible to the public and the church felt like it would be a wonderful outreach to the community to have a court that was always open to the public. Most days during the summer there were people of all ages playing on the court.
