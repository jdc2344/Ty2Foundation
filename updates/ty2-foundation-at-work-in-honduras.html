Ty2 Foundation At Work In Honduras - June 2012

Through the Ty2Foundation, a family in Honduras was recently blessed with the building of a brand new home. Once living in a shack, they now have a roof over their heads. Ty would have been honored to bless a family with a great home.








Ty’s foundation helped sponsor a recent trip through Jovenes en Camino to serve at an orphanage in Honduras. While there, volunteers painted a mural and the boys staying in the orphanage put their names and handprints on the mural. It was then dedicated to Ty’s memory.
