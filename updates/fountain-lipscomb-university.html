Fountain @ Lipscomb University - June 2013

The Ty2 Foundation was able to donate to complete a beautiful fountain in the middle of Lipscomb University’s campus. The fountain is symmetrical with one side built to be used as a baptistery. There have been many baptisms in the waters of the fountain since it opened in June, 2013. In fact, Lipscomb University is Ty and Nancy’s alma mater. They were engaged very near the sight of the fountain!

There are engraved limestone markers on the fountain. One says, “Dedicated to our son, brother and friend, Ty Osman II, 1993-2012, another says, “Generosity of the Ty2 Foundation” and on one side across the entire fountain it reads, “My flesh and my heart may fail but God is the strength of my heart and my portion forever.” Psalm 73:26

We hope you will be able to go by the campus and see the fountain. It is a stunning focal point in the center of Lipscomb University’s campus.
