Hard Bargain Center renamed Ty’s House

FRANKLIN — A house that once was the residence of the caretaker of Mt. Hope Cemetery will be dedicated on Wednesday with a ribbon cutting ceremony for what will be the Hard Bargain Association’s new Community Center & Office.

The building, renamed Ty’s House, was renovated by Solomon Builders, the Ty 2 Foundation and an army of volunteers in memory of Ty Osman II.

The ribbon cutting is set for 10 a.m. Wednesday at the house, 608 Mt. Hope St.
