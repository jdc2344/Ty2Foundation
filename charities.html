Charities

Ty had a servant’s heart. He was always finding ways to reach out to people around him. The organizations below are some of the charities the Ty2 Foundation has supported. Through this foundation, Ty’s work continues.

Both Hands Foundation

James tells us that pure and undefiled religion is serving widows and orphans. Both Hands Foundation is an organization that brings those words to life. Ty loved serving through Both Hands. It gave him opportunities to serve people and be outside—two of his favorite things. The Ty Osman II Big Build project for Both Hands has already raised more than $60,000 from nearly 300 donors that will be directed to an orphanage in Honduras.

JH Ranch

A great family doesn't just happen. It takes hard work, sacrifice and selflessness. During their time at JH Ranch, Ty and his family built spiritually strong, faith-based bonds. The ranch allows parents and children access to an increasingly scarce commodity: time together. Ty's foundation will afford that same opportunity to others.

Jovenes en Camino

Ty was blessed to be a part of a loving, encouraging family. And he had a deep compassion for those less fortunate. Through Jovenes en Camino, he helped to support orphans, showing them a genuine love and true support. He was a phenomenal son and brother. And he loved sharing the blessing of family with others.

Hard Bargain Association

For over 100 years, Hard Bargain, an African American neighborhood in Franklin, Tennessee has been fighting to be preserved. Through the HBA, homes are restored and built to help revitalize this generations-old neighborhood.
